Our History

The Journey of VelvetGlow

Founded in Kolkata, VelvetGlow Creams Pvt. Ltd. began as a passion project of a small group of skin scientists and organic chemists. Our mission was simple: to create premium, luxury-grade face creams and body moisturizers without compromising on chemical safety or environmental ethics.

Over the years, we have expanded our research laboratories to refine standard skin care formulas, incorporating natural plant-based lipids, natural vitamins, and sunscreen factors. Today, we are proud to offer a highly successful product line featuring deep skin moisturizers, brightening creams, and broad-spectrum sunscreens that suit various skin tones and weather conditions.

From formulation to bottling, our standards remain absolute. We select only the highest grade botanicals, test rigorously in dermatological laboratories, and bottle in eco-friendly glass containers to deliver the purest, most luxurious skincare products available on the market.

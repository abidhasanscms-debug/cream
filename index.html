Skincare FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our products? Here are answers to our most common inquiries.

Are VelvetGlow products suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, all of our products are specifically formulated to be gentle yet effective. They are dermatologically tested, hypoallergenic, and free from harsh synthetic fragrances, parabens, and sulfates, making them highly suitable for sensitive skin types.

How often should I use the Radiant Glow Face Cream?

For best results, we recommend applying the Radiant Glow Face Cream twice daily. Apply it in the morning after cleansing (before applying sunscreen) and as the final step in your evening skincare routine to allow deep absorption overnight.

Are your ingredients ethically sourced and cruelty-free?

Absolutely. VelvetGlow is committed to cruelty-free beauty. We do not test our final products or individual ingredients on animals at any stage of development. Additionally, we source all raw botanicals from eco-certified sustainable farms.
